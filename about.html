About Our King Street Coffee Shop

A simple, real-feeling coffee and food concept built around downtown Charleston.

A downtown Charleston coffee shop concept

King Street Coffee & Kitchen is built around what people expect from a true coffee shop in Charleston, SC. It combines fresh coffee, simple food options, and a comfortable setting that works for everyday use in a busy downtown environment.

Located along King Street, the idea behind the shop is to reflect the natural flow of Charleston. That means early mornings with coffee, mid-day lunch breaks, and relaxed afternoons where people can sit, meet, or recharge.

The focus is not on overcomplication, but on consistency—good drinks, approachable food, and a space that feels like it belongs in the Charleston coffee scene.

What this coffee shop is built around

Coffee First

A strong focus on espresso drinks, iced coffee, and simple café staples that work all day in downtown Charleston.

Easy Food Options

Breakfast and lunch items designed for convenience, whether someone is walking King Street or stopping in to sit.

Local Feel

A space that fits Charleston’s downtown culture, appealing to locals, students, and visitors alike.

Part of the King Street experience

King Street is one of the most recognized areas in Charleston, and a coffee shop here needs to feel like it belongs. That means being easy to walk into, easy to order from, and easy to enjoy.

The shop fits naturally into a day on King Street—whether someone is shopping, heading to class near the College of Charleston, or just exploring downtown.

It is designed to be a reliable stop for coffee, food, and a break from the pace of the city without losing that Charleston energy.
